Overview

Legnica is a city located in the southwest of poland upon the river Kaczawa, also known as Katzbach in english, it is quite an old city so it is full of historical architecture dating back hundreds of years, making it a great place to be able to admire the past.

On top of being a hub of historical wonder, it is a great spot to visit, there are plenty of facilities to accomodate tourists as well as no shortage of spots to visit

The citys charm lies in its highly religious history, reflected in the many buildings dedicated to religious practise, with churches and cathedrals scattered about, a large number of which tower spectacularly high despite their age.
