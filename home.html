Overview

Legnica is a city located in the southwest of poland upon the river Kaczawa, also known as Katzbach in english, it is quite an old city so it is full of historical architecture some of which dates back to medieval times such as the Cathedral of saints Peter and Paul, making it a great place to be able to admire the past.

Some notable places include Piast castle, the Herring Houses, and even the market square, all of which hold significant cultural backgrounds and history.

There are also many activities that one can enjoy such as the Library and Archives, which preserve the citys historical and cultural records, allowing anyone to learn about its origins, or one can visit the city park, which is the largest park in legnica, to sit back and enjoy the greenery.
